Get Instant Fashion Advice

Do you often feel spoilt for choice while shopping? ​Is​ Vogue is your bible, ​dressing up, your way of life, and sharing your #OOTD and #OOTN ​looks with your friends a daily habit​? Well, then​ StyleDotMe is the perfect fashion app for you​!​

INSTANT POLLS

The app allows you to instantly poll your friends and followers and get them to help you choose an outfit or look, by voting for your options.

LIVE TIMER

If you need help to make a choice urgently, a live timer can be attached to your SOS notification that will count down to remind friends of your deadline.

EXPERT FASHION ADVICE

Styledotme has a panel of fashion bloggers and stylists who will give instant advice to the users when they find a post with a live timer.

FOLLOWING

The app allows you to instantly poll your friends and followers and get them to help you choose an outfit or look, by voting for your options.

SEAMLESS INTEGRATION WITH INSTAGRAM

You can import your posts from Instagram with a single tap. Now your post on styledotme can be automatically shared with your followers in Instagram.

PRIVACY

In the latest update you also have the option of blocking/unblocking users to make your experience on the app a fuss-free and enjoyable one .
