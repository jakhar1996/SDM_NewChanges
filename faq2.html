What is Styledotme?

Styledotme is community for people who loves fashion, who loves to dress up and show it to their friends and take advice from them on how they look in various outfits, which dress of shoes will look best on them etc. The Styledotme app, which is only android based right now, allows you to click pictures and get ratings from your friends and people following you. If a person is confused what to wear, what to shop, which color, which style, they can create a poll and share with everyone in their network. If there is an urgency one can also put a timer for a particular time and the notification will be send to all your followers who will help you make better decision in choosing. You can add image-enhancing filters to the photos you click. You can also share your posts with your friends on other social networking platforms such as Facebook and Twitter. You are able to “follow” your friends, favorite celebrities, brands and bloggers.

Who is Styledotme meant for?

Styledotme is meant for anyone who is 13 years+ and loves to dress up.
